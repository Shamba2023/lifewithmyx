Life with my X

How my X and I travel the world


Still. Silent. Seen.

Photography is the art of seeing—capturing fleeting moments and transforming them into timeless stories. It’s exciting because it blends creativity with perspective in a single frame. Whether it’s the quiet drama of a misty landscape or the vibrant pulse of a city street, a photographer explores the world with curiosity and intention.

My X is my Fuji X-T5 camera that I carry everywhere I visit. I don't judge if it is a good camera or a bad one, I feel it is my close companion. Explore the world with my X and me!
